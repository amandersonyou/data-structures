Temperature Sensor

For one of the Data Structures final projects, I tracked the interior temperature of my apartment in Brooklyn, New York for over a month. To do this, I first assembled a Particle Photon sensor and placed it in my kitchen which is also a main thoroughfare near the entry and bathroom containing a boiler pipe. I collected data every minute from November 1st, 2019 until December 8th, 2019.

In the visual below, I'm choosing to set the y-axis from 75 to 84 degrees as the temperature is always within that range. As you can see, the temperature fluctuates often, likely based upon the additional heat absorbed by the exterior temperature.
